Research Datasets

Our research datasets provide useful information about how we can achieve a more sustainable future. They cover topics such as climate goals, support for electric vehicles and renewable energy, and regulations to protect the environment for energy production and infrastructure. We host these datasets on Zenodo, making them easy to find, check, and use for research by anyone interested.
