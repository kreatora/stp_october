Welcome!

We are pleased to present our Renewable Energy (RE) Policy Ontology, developed as a contribution to the NFDI4Energy project. Our primary aim was to create a structured, semantic framework that enhances the understanding, comparison, and analysis of diverse renewable energy policies, making complex information more accessible and interoperable.
